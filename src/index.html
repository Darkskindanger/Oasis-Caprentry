Decking

The backyard began to transform into an extension of the indoor living area, with the deck serving as a bridge between the two. In the 1980s, decks became widely popular as people aimed to expand their living spaces. Initially, these decks were basic wooden platforms, frequently made from locally sourced timber.

As the idea of outdoor living became more popular, these platforms developed into elaborate structures featuring outdoor kitchens and stylish furniture, tailored to the Australian climate. Since then, decks have continued to evolve, varying in size and serving a wide range of purposes.
